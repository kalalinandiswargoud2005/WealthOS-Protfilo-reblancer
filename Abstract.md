# Abstract

Managing a diversified investment portfolio across multiple, volatile asset classes—such as equities, mutual funds, cryptocurrencies, and precious metals—demands constant vigilance and disciplined rebalancing. Retail investors frequently struggle to maintain their desired risk profiles due to the time required for continuous market monitoring, the complexity of cross-asset valuation, and the influence of emotional bias during market fluctuations. As asset prices shift, portfolios experience "drift," where the actual asset allocation deviates from the investor's original target weights. While traditional robo-advisors offer rigid, rule-based rebalancing, and manual platforms require constant user intervention, there is a critical need for an intelligent, adaptable, and autonomous solution.

**WealthOS** is an autonomous, AI-driven wealth management platform designed to solve these challenges by fully automating portfolio rebalancing and cross-asset allocation. The platform aggregates real-time market data from diverse, live sources (including CoinGecko, MFAPI, and Metals.dev) to establish a unified baseline for portfolio valuation. Using this data, the system continuously monitors the portfolio's drift index against predefined user target allocations.

The core innovation of WealthOS lies in its **Agentic AI Rebalancing Engine**, powered by on-device Large Language Models (LLMs) such as Qwen 2.5 Coder and Llama 3.2 running via an Ollama runtime environment. By utilizing on-device processing, the system ensures maximum financial data privacy while retaining powerful reasoning capabilities. Operating on customizable execution schedules, the AI agent autonomously evaluates market conditions, identifies critical portfolio drift (e.g., deviations exceeding a 0.4% threshold), calculates the precise capital required for correction, and automatically executes simulated buy/sell orders to restore optimal asset distribution.

To ensure trust and demystify the AI's autonomous actions, WealthOS features a transparent **Chain-of-Thought Audit Ledger**. Rather than operating as a "black box," the AI logs its exact step-by-step reasoning for every executed trade. Users are provided with a comprehensive dashboard that features live order books, transaction ledgers, interactive performance analytics, and clear documentation of how and why the AI decided to intervene. 

Ultimately, the deployment of WealthOS creates a highly efficient, self-managing financial ecosystem. It not only minimizes portfolio drift and optimizes asset allocation without user intervention but also establishes a new industry standard for transparency and user control in automated wealth management.
